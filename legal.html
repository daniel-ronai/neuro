Who we are

NeuroLoop is a social intelligence audit service operated by Daniel Ronai ("I", "me", "my"). When you submit the order form on this website, you are sharing information directly with me. You can reach me at hello@danielsends.email.

What information I collect

When you fill out the order form, I collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your brand or store name
  • The URLs and social profile links you submit
  • Any optional context you provide about your goals
  • The date and time of submission

I do not collect payment information directly. Any payment processing is handled by a third-party provider and is subject to their privacy policy.

How I use your information

The information you submit is used solely to:

  • Carry out the audit you requested — scraping and analysing the public social content at the links you provide
  • Deliver your report and communicate with you about it
  • Answer follow-up questions included in your order

I do not use your information for advertising or any purpose unrelated to fulfilling your order.

What data the audit involves

The audit analyses publicly available content — comments, captions, and reviews posted on the social profiles and pages you link to. I do not access any private accounts, direct messages, or any data that is not publicly visible.

Who I share your data with

I do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their own purposes. Your information may pass through the following tools as part of delivering the service:

  • A webhook automation service (e.g. Make or Zapier) to receive your form submission
  • Email to deliver your report

These tools process your data only as necessary to deliver the service.

How long I keep your data

I retain your submission details and the associated report for up to 12 months in case you have follow-up questions. After that, your personal information is deleted. You can request earlier deletion at any time by emailing me.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data I hold about you
  • Ask me to correct or delete your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time (this does not affect any processing already completed)

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@danielsends.email. I will respond within 30 days.

Cookies
